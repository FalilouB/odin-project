Ice Cream Pie

Description

Ice cream pie is a frozen dessert made by spreading softened ice cream into a pie crust - usually a cookie or graham cracker crust — and freezing it until firm. It’s often topped with whipped cream, chocolate sauce, or other sweet toppings before serving.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups crushed chocolate cookies
  • 5 tbsp melted butter
  • 1.5 quarts (6 cups) of your favorite ice cream, softened slightly
  • Optional: chocolate chips, nuts, cookie chunks, or caramel swirls
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Chocolate syrup or ganache (optional)
  • Crushed nuts or sprinkles (optional)
  • Cherries (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix cookie crumbs and melted butter in a bowl until it resembles wet sand.
  2. Press firmly into a 9-inch pie dish (bottom and sides).
  3. Freeze for 10–15 minutes while you prep the filling.
  4. Let the ice cream sit out for 10–15 minutes until soft but not melted.
  5. Scoop and spread the ice cream into the chilled crust, pressing it in evenly.
  6. Add mix-ins (if using) as you layer the ice cream.
  7. Cover with foil or plastic wrap.
  8. Freeze for at least 4 hours or overnight until solid.
  9. Just before serving, top with whipped cream, drizzle with chocolate syrup, and add sprinkles or nuts.
  10. Let sit for a few minutes at room temp for easier slicing.
  11. Cut into wedges and serve cold.
